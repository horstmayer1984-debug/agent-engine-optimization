# Agent Engine Optimization

The next layer of digital optimization is not about being cited. It is about being used.

Most of what the market calls AEO today is Answer Engine Optimization, making content visible in AI-generated answers. That is important, but it is only the read layer.

The execution layer is different. It is where autonomous AI agents do not just read your content, they act on it. They book, purchase, compare, orchestrate, and transact. And they need a fundamentally different kind of digital infrastructure to do so.

Agent Engine Optimization is the discipline of making websites, APIs, data structures, and digital services readable, trustworthy, and actionable for autonomous AI agents.
