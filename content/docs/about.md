This site is a research hub and strategy resource for Agent Engine Optimization, the discipline of making websites, APIs, and digital infrastructure readable, trustworthy, and actionable for autonomous AI agents.

## What this site covers

The core focus is the distinction between the read layer (content that AI systems can find and cite) and the [execution layer](/docs/execution-layer/) (infrastructure that AI agents can act through). Most of what the market calls AEO today addresses the read layer. This site covers both, with particular depth on the execution layer that most resources ignore.

Topics include AEO frameworks and methodology, protocol analysis (UCP, MCP, A2A, AP2), industry-specific AEO strategies for ecommerce, B2B SaaS, support, and marketing, technical implementation guides, and market analysis of the agentic web.

## Who this is for

Technical SEOs and content strategists who need to understand how AI agents interact with websites. CTOs and product managers evaluating agent-readiness for their digital infrastructure. Ecommerce and SaaS leaders preparing for autonomous purchasing and procurement agents.

## Methodology

All content on this site follows a principle of operational specificity over conceptual abstraction. Recommendations are based on published protocol specifications, real platform announcements, and observable market behavior rather than speculation.

Where information is uncertain or forward-looking, it is marked as such. Assumptions are labeled explicitly.
