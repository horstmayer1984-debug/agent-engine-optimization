Perplexity SEO is the practice of creating clear, source-worthy content that can support AI-generated answers with citations. It belongs to the broader field of [Generative Engine Optimization](/docs/generative-engine-optimization/), with a specific emphasis: being useful enough to be referenced.

Citation-oriented AI search rewards pages that are direct, reliable and easy to verify. Vague content is weak. Shallow content is weak. A page must give the system something worth citing.

## What makes content citation-worthy

Citation-worthy content answers a specific question, includes original or well-structured information, makes claims that are easy to verify, and is organized so the relevant passage can be extracted.

A generic article that repeats common statements is less useful than a page with definitions, criteria, examples and comparison tables.

## Structure matters

Put definitions near the top. Use headings that describe the content. Use tables for comparisons. Add concise summaries before deeper explanations. A page about "GEO vs AEO" should state the distinction immediately, then expand.

## Use original frameworks

Original frameworks make your page more memorable and more useful. For this site, one strong framework is: SEO gets you found, Answer Engine Optimization gets you answered, GEO gets you cited, Agent Engine Optimization gets you used.

## Connect to Agent Engine Optimization

Perplexity-style visibility creates attention, but attention is not completion. Citation strategy should connect to action strategy. A cited buying guide should link to structured product data. A cited audit guide should link to the [readiness checker](/tools/aeo-readiness-checker.html).
