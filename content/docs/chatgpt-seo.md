ChatGPT SEO is the practice of making your brand, content and expertise easier to understand and reference in AI-assisted conversations. It is not a guaranteed way to force inclusion in any specific answer. It is a strategy for becoming clearer, more useful and more credible.

Users ask AI assistants broad, conversational questions. They may ask for explanations, recommendations, comparisons, checklists or decisions. Your content has to be useful enough to support those moments.

## What changes in conversational discovery

Traditional search starts with a keyword. AI conversations start with a problem. Instead of "CRM software comparison," a user may ask: "I run a 50-person B2B SaaS company. Which CRM tools should I compare if I care about integrations, reporting and implementation speed?"

This type of query needs structured, contextual content. Generic pages are less helpful.

## Build content around questions

ChatGPT SEO should start with real user questions: what is this concept, how is it different from alternatives, when should I use it, what are the risks, what steps should I follow, how do I measure success, what comes next.

## Make expertise explicit

AI systems need signals that content is specific and reliable. Make expertise visible through author context, clear methodology, original frameworks, examples, current dates and transparent limitations.

## Prepare for agentic follow-up

AI conversations increasingly lead to action. A user may ask for a summary, then ask the assistant to find options, compare them and take a next step. ChatGPT SEO should connect to [Agent Engine Optimization](/docs/what-is-aeo/). Content should not only explain. It should lead to structured tools, forms, policies or action paths.
